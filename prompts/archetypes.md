**Objective**: Versatility and Consistency

The Balance archetype seeks stability and the ability to handle almost any threat. It is the middle ground between defense and offense.

**Typical Composition**: A healthy mix of 2-3 Walls (physical and special), 1-2 Utility Pivots, 1 Cleric or Support, and 1-2 Sweepers or Wallbreakers.

**Playstyle**: Based on gradually wearing down the opponent while maintaining the health of its own Pok√©mon. Uses Pivots to gain momentum and bring in attackers at the opportune moment.

**Weaknesses**: May lack the brute force to break through extreme Walls or the speed to stop a well-executed Hyper Offense.

## üí• Offense

**Objective**: Pressure, Pressure, Pressure

Offense focuses on overwhelming the opponent with constant damage and quick KOs.

**Typical Composition**: Few or no Walls. High number of Wallbreakers, Sweepers (often with Choice Items), 1 aggressive Hazard Setter, and often a Revenge Killer.

**Playstyle**: Seeks to establish hazards quickly and then have attackers enter and eliminate threats before the opponent can respond. The game is often decided in the first few turns.

**Strengths**: Capable of generating an enormous amount of damage and pressuring the opponent, finishing games very quickly.

**Weaknesses**: Very susceptible to passive damage from its own entry hazards and can be stopped if its main Sweeper is neutralized or defeated.

## üõ°Ô∏è Bulky Offense

**Objective**: Attack with Resistance

An evolution of Offense that incorporates bulk to ensure that attackers can take a hit and return damage.

**Typical Composition**: Wallbreakers and Sweepers that have decent defensive stats (bulk). Includes very bulky Utility Pivots and Walls with offensive capability (e.g., Utility Walls with status moves).

**Playstyle**: Attacks more slowly than pure Offense. The team can trade hits without falling immediately, which gives more opportunities to predict and set up.

**Strengths**: High damage output combined with longevity. Less vulnerable to Revenge Killers and entry pressure.

**Weaknesses**: Slower than pure Offense. Can still be worn down by dedicated Stall teams if it doesn't have a strong Wallbreaker.

## üê¢ Stall / Defense

**Objective**: Wear Down and Survive

The Stall archetype's main objective is to outlast the opponent through recovery, status denial, and passive damage.

**Typical Composition**: Most are dedicated Walls (Physical, Special, and Mixed), Clerics, Phazers, and Hazard Removers/Setters (who use Toxic Spikes or Spikes).

**Playstyle**: Minimizes the opponent's attacking opportunities using status moves (Toxic), Phazing to accumulate hazard damage, and constantly healing. They seek to nullify the opposing Sweeper at all costs.

**Strengths**: Extremely difficult to break if played correctly. Neutralizes many common attackers.

**Weaknesses**: Very passive and predictable. Very vulnerable to Wallbreakers or Trappers that can eliminate key Walls. Mistakes in gameplay are heavily penalized.
